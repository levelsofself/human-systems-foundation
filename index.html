Human Systems Foundation

The Human Systems Project is an initiative of Human Systems Foundation.

Delivering workforce development, behavioral health, youth empowerment, and community technology programs for underserved communities through structured human performance and systems-based interventions.

Workforce Development

Skills training, job placement, and reentry programs designed to create sustainable career pathways and reduce recidivism.

Youth Development

Empowerment, intervention, and mentorship programs supporting at-risk and justice-involved youth.

Behavioral Health

Community-based programs that improve outcomes through integrated support, technology, and human-centered care.

Technology Access

Expanding digital access and preparing communities for responsible participation in an AI-driven economy.

Current Initiatives

Currently establishing pilot programs in Los Angeles County focused on workforce readiness, youth mentorship, community behavioral health support, and digital access. Actively seeking partnerships with community organizations, agencies, and funders.
